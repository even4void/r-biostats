rats.dta

Experimental study of the gain in weight of rats fed on four different diets, defined by amount (low / high) and by source of protein (beef and cereal).

3 variables : Weight.Gain (gain de poids en g), Diet.Amount (intensité de régime, low/high) et Diet.Type (type de régime, beef/cereal)

Fichier au format RData.

Références :

Hand, D. J., Daly, F., McConway, K., & Ostrowski, E. (Éds). (1993). A Handbook of Small Data Sets. New York : Chapman & Hall/CRC Press.  http://www.stat.ncsu.edu/research/sas/sicl/data/
