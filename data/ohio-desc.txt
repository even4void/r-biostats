
ohio.rda

A longitudinal study of the health effects of air pollution. 

4 variables : maternal smoking (smoke, considered fixed), time (age), wheezing status (resp, 1=oui 0=non) et identifiant (id) 

Fichier au format RData

- Fitzmaurice, G. and Laird, N. (1993). A likelihood-based method for analysing longitudinal binary response. Biometrika, 80(1), 141–151.
